Senior Seminar: Evolutionary Medicine
Core Reading List
(last updated Mar. 10, 2022)

Course Text

The only required course text, Why We Get Sick, will be used throughout the semester and serves as a good reference texts. The notes and bibliography in WWGS are excellent and provide a launching point for searching the literature.

1. Nesse RM, Williams GC. 1996. Why We Get Sick: The New Science of Darwinian Medicine, Vintage Press, NY.

Core Readings for Class
(Core Readings Available at the Course D2L Website)

The Core Readings will form the basis for discussion in class. At a minimum, students should read the Core Readings, but other readings will also be made available. Student presenters/discussion leaders and, hopefully, other students will go beyond the Core Readings to construct their own understanding of the topics to be discussed. This is the true spirit of a "seminar".
